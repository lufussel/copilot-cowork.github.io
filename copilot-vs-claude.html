⚔️ Compete

Competing With Claude

How to position M365 Copilot when Claude comes up in customer conversations — honestly, confidently, and with conviction.

🔗Integration is the game-changer
💡Claude is brilliant in a vacuum. It has no idea who your CEO is, what's in your pipeline, or what was decided in last Thursday's Teams call.
✅Copilot knows your org. Microsoft Graph connects Copilot to emails, meetings, Teams chats, SharePoint, and the org chart — giving answers grounded in YOUR reality, not generic world knowledge.
🎯Ask the customer: "Can Claude tell you what your top customer raised as a concern in Tuesday's call?" Copilot can.
🔒Enterprise-grade trust & compliance
⚠️Direct Claude API usage triggers procurement, InfoSec review, and data residency questions — "where does our data actually go?"
✅Copilot inherits Microsoft's compliance posture: Purview, data residency, eDiscovery, DLP, sensitivity labels — all managed in the admin centre your IT team already controls.
🎯The security team already trusts Microsoft. They need a separate review cycle for Anthropic. That's months of delay and risk.
⚡Actions, not just advice
📝Claude tells you what to write. Copilot writes it, formats it, sends it, and tracks it — all within the apps your people live in every day.
✅Copilot takes action: books meetings, summarises long threads, generates Excel models, creates polished decks, drafts and sends emails — with one click.
🎯ROI is concrete and measurable: time saved per user per week, easily quantified and included in value realisations.
🏗️Platform + ecosystem advantage
🔧Copilot Studio lets customers build custom agents grounded in their own business data — SAP, Salesforce, ServiceNow, and hundreds of connectors — no custom dev required.
✅Azure AI Foundry gives enterprise MLOps, model governance, and the ability to bring any model — including Claude — under one enterprise roof.
🎯The position: "You don't just get an AI. You get the best AI platform for enterprise — and it can incorporate Claude too."
💰Total cost of ownership
📊Claude API costs scale fast — token-based pricing means enterprise-wide usage adds up quickly, and requires dev investment to integrate with existing systems.
✅Copilot is predictable per-user-per-month on top of existing M365 licences. No infrastructure overhead, no shadow IT, no surprise bills.
🎯Run the maths: enabling 10,000 users on Claude API vs Copilot — total cost including integration, security review, and ongoing support is usually a compelling story.
🤝When to acknowledge Claude's strengths
✅Be honest: Claude's reasoning depth and coding ability are genuinely impressive. Technical buyers will know — don't dismiss it or you'll lose credibility instantly.
🔄Reframe: "The good news is Microsoft and Anthropic are partners. Azure AI Foundry gives you Claude with Microsoft's enterprise compliance. You don't have to choose."
🎯The winning play: Don't lose the platform conversation by over-defending the model. You're selling a productivity ecosystem — and you win that game easily.

💬 Prompt Library

Example Prompts for the Account Team

Copy, paste, and adapt for Cowork. Swap the highlighted bits for your own customer, situation, and context.

🔍 Pre-Meeting Prep
Research [customer name] ahead of my meeting tomorrow. Pull their latest news, any mentions of AI or digital transformation in the press, and check my emails and Teams chats for recent context from their team. Summarise in 5 bullet points I can scan before the call.
📧 Post-Meeting Follow-Up
I've just finished a meeting with [customer name]. Draft a follow-up email that: thanks them for their time, recaps the 3 key things we discussed, confirms the next steps we agreed, and includes a soft call-to-action to keep momentum. Keep it professional but warm — British English please.
📊 Relationship Health Check
Go through all my emails and Teams messages from [customer name] in the last 30 days. Flag any risks, concerns, or blockers they've raised — and any signals suggesting they might be looking at a competitor. Give me a one-paragraph health summary of the relationship.
🏆 Executive Briefing
My VP is joining my next call with [customer name]. Create a one-page brief they can read in 5 minutes: who the key stakeholders are, where we are in the sales cycle, what the customer cares about most, and what outcome we want from this meeting.
📅 Monday Morning Triage
It's Monday morning. Go through my unread emails from the last 3 days. Prioritise them for me: flag anything urgent that needs a reply today, highlight any customer escalations, and draft a quick acknowledgement for anything that just needs a holding response.
💼 Business Case Builder
Help me build a business case for M365 Copilot for [customer name]. They have [X] users, are in [industry], and their top priorities are [saving time / reducing costs / improving compliance]. Give me 3 ROI scenarios I can present to their CFO.
⚔️ Technical Battle Card
Create a technical comparison of Azure OpenAI Service vs [AWS Bedrock / Google Vertex AI / direct Anthropic API] for an enterprise customer evaluating AI platforms. Cover: model availability, data residency, compliance certifications, fine-tuning options, pricing model, and MLOps capabilities. Format as a side-by-side table.
🔍 Technical Discovery Mining
Search all my Teams chats and emails with [customer name]'s technical team over the last 60 days. Extract every technical requirement, concern, or objection they've raised about AI. Group them by theme (security, data, integration, cost) and suggest which Microsoft products or features address each one.
🏗️ Architecture Workshop Prep
I'm running a half-day AI architecture workshop with [customer name]'s CTO and platform engineering team. They're evaluating build vs buy for LLM infrastructure. Create a workshop agenda, 5 discovery questions I should ask, and a one-page Azure AI Foundry capability summary to leave behind.
📐 POC Design
Help me design a 4-week Copilot Studio proof of concept for [customer name]. They want an internal knowledge assistant connecting to their HR SharePoint and IT ServiceNow instance. Define success criteria, architecture overview, week-by-week plan, and what Microsoft resources I should engage.
🎓 Developer Enablement
Design a half-day Azure AI Foundry hands-on lab for [customer name]'s developer team of ~20 people. They're experienced Python developers with REST API knowledge but no Azure AI background. Include learning objectives, 3 lab exercises, and key takeaways. Make it practical — they want to deploy something by end of day.
